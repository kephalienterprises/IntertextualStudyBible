\chapter{La Ruina de Babilonia}
\lettrine[lines=3,slope=-0.5em,loversize=0.1]{\textcolor{red}{Y}}{\ } vino uno de los siete ángeles que tenían las siete copas, y habló conmigo, diciéndome: Ven acá, y te mostraré la condenación de la grande ramera, la cual está sentada sobre muchas aguas:%
	\cbiblefoot{Jeremiah}{51:13}{O thou that dwellest upon many waters, abundant in treasures, thine end is come, the measure of thy covetousness}
\vnum{2}con la cual han fornicado los reyes de la tierra,%
	\cbiblefoot{Isaiah}{23:17}{And it shall come to pass after the end of seventy years, that Jehovah will visit Tyre, and she shall return to her hire, and shall play the harlot with all the kingdoms of the world upon the face of the earth}
 y los que moran en la tierra se han embriagado con el vino%
	\cbiblefootduosb{Jeremiah}{25:15}{For thus saith Jehovah, the God of Israel, unto me: Take this cup of the wine of wrath at my hand, and cause all the nations, to whom I send thee, to drink it}%
					  {51:7}{Babylon hath been a golden cup in Jehovah’s hand, that made all the earth drunken: the nations have drunk of her wine; therefore the nations are mad}
 de su fornicación.%
	\cbiblefoot{Nahum}{3:4}{because of the multitude of the whoredoms of the well-favored harlot, the mistress of witchcrafts, that selleth nations through her whoredoms, and families through her witchcrafts}
\vnum{3}Y me llevó en Espíritu al desierto; 

y vi una mujer sentada sobre una bestia bermeja llena de nombres de blasfemia y que tenía siete cabezas y diez cuernos. 
\vnum{4}Y la mujer estaba vestida de púrpura y de escarlata, y dorada con oro, y adornada de piedras preciosas y de perlas,%
	\cbiblefoot{Ezekiel}{28:13}{Thou wast in Eden, the garden of God; every precious stone was thy covering, the sardius, the topaz, and the diamond, the beryl, the onyx, and the jasper, the sapphire, the emerald, and the carbuncle, and gold: the workmanship of thy tabrets and of thy pipes was in thee; in the day that thou wast created they were prepared}
 teniendo un cáliz de oro en su mano lleno de abominaciones y de la suciedad de su fornicación; 
\vnum{5}y en su frente un nombre escrito:%
\begin{verse}
\textsc{misterio, babilonia la grande,\\ 
la madre de las fornicaciones\\ 
y de las abominaciones de la tierra.}
\end{verse}
\vnum{6}Y vi la mujer embriagada de la sangre de los santos, y de la sangre de los mártires de Jesús: y cuando la vi, quedé maravillado de grande admiración. 

\vnum{7}Y el ángel me dijo: ¿Por qué te maravillas? Yo te diré el misterio de la mujer, y de la bestia que la trae, la cual tiene siete cabezas y diez cuernos. 
\vnum{8}La bestia que has visto, fué, y no es; y ha de subir del abismo, y ha de ir á perdición: y los moradores de la tierra, cuyos nombres no están escritos en el libro de la vida desde la fundación del mundo, se maravillarán viendo la bestia que era y no es, aunque es. 
\vnum{9}Y aquí hay mente que tiene sabiduría. Las siete cabezas son siete montes, sobre los cuales se asienta la mujer. 
\vnum{10}Y son siete reyes. Los cinco son caídos; el uno es, el otro aun no es venido; y cuando viniere, es necesario que dure breve tiempo. 
\vnum{11}Y la bestia que era, y no es, es también el octavo, y es de los siete, y va á perdición. 
\vnum{12}Y los diez cuernos que has visto, son diez reyes,%
	\cbiblefoot{Daniel}{7:20, 24}{And concerning the ten horns that were on its head, and the other horn which came up, and before which three fell, even that horn that had eyes, and a mouth that spake great things, whose look was more stout than its fellows\ldots And as for the ten horns, out of this kingdom shall ten kings arise: and another shall arise after them; and he shall be diverse from the former, and he shall put down three kings}
 que aun no han recibido reino; mas tomarán potencia por una hora como reyes con la bestia. 
\vnum{13}Estos tienen un consejo, y darán su potencia y autoridad á la bestia. 
\vnum{14}Ellos pelearán contra el Cordero, y el Cordero los vencerá, porque es el Señor de los señores, y el Rey de los reyes:%
	\cbiblefoottrio{Deuteronomy}{10:17}{For Jehovah your God, he is God of gods, and Lord of lords, the great God, the mighty, and the terrible, who regardeth not persons, nor taketh reward}%
				  {Psalms}{136:3}{Oh give thanks unto the Lord of lords; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever}%
				   {Daniel}{2:47}{The king answered unto Daniel, and said, Of a truth your God is the God of gods, and the Lord of kings, and a revealer of secrets, seeing thou hast been able to reveal this secret}
 y los que están con él son llamados, y elegidos, y fieles. 

\vnum{15}Y él me dice: Las aguas que has visto donde la ramera se sienta, son pueblos y muchedumbres y naciones y lenguas.
\vnum{16}Y los diez cuernos que viste en la bestia, éstos aborrecerán á la ramera, y la harán desolada%
	\cbiblefoot{Ezekiel}{26:19-21}{For thus saith the Lord Jehovah: When I shall make thee a desolate city, like the cities that are not inhabited; when I shall bring up the deep upon thee, and the great waters shall cover thee; then will I bring thee down with them that descend into the pit, to the people of old time, and will make thee to dwell in the nether parts of the earth, in the places that are desolate of old, with them that go down to the pit, that thou be not inhabited; and I will set glory in the land of the living. I will make thee a terror, and thou shalt no more have any being; though thou be sought for, yet shalt thou never be found again, saith the Lord Jehovah}
 y desnuda:%
	\footnote{\cbibleref{Ezekiel}{16:37-39}{Therefore behold, I will gather all thy lovers, with whom thou hast taken pleasure, and all them that thou hast loved, with all them that thou hast hated; I will even gather them against thee on every side, and will uncover thy nakedness unto them, that they may see all thy nakedness. And I will judge thee, as women that break wedlock and shed blood are judged; and I will bring upon thee the blood of wrath and jealousy. I will also give thee into their hand, and they shall throw down thy vaulted place, and break down thy lofty places; and they shall strip thee of thy clothes, and take thy fair jewels; and they shall leave thee naked and bare} %
				\cbiblechvs{Ezekiel}{23:26-29}{They shall also strip thee of thy clothes, and take away thy fair jewels. Thus will I make thy lewdness to cease from thee, and thy whoredom brought from the land of Egypt; so that thou shalt not lift up thine eyes unto them, nor remember Egypt any more. For thus saith the Lord Jehovah: Behold, I will deliver thee into the hand of them whom thou hatest, into the hand of them from whom thy soul is alienated; and they shall deal with thee in hatred, and shall take away all thy labor, and shall leave thee naked and bare; and the nakedness of thy whoredoms shall be uncovered, both thy lewdness and thy whoredoms} %
				\cbibleref{Hosea}{2:2-3}{Contend with your mother, contend; for she is not my wife, neither am I her husband; and let her put away her whoredoms from her face, and her adulteries from between her breasts; lest I strip her naked, and set her as in the day that she was born, and make her as a wilderness, and set her like a dry land, and slay her with thirst}}
 y comerán sus carnes, y la quemarán con fuego:
	\cbiblefootduo{Jeremiah}{34:22}{Behold, I will command, saith Jehovah, and cause them to return to this city; and they shall fight against it, and take it, and burn it with fire: and I will make the cities of Judah a desolation, without inhabitant}%
		{Leviticus}{21:9}{And the daughter of any priest, if she profane herself by playing the harlot, she profaneth her father: she shall be burnt with fire}
\vnum{17}Porque Dios ha puesto en sus corazones ejecutar lo que le plugo, y el ponerse de acuerdo, y dar su reino á la bestia, hasta que sean cumplidas las palabras de Dios. 
\vnum{18}Y la mujer que has visto, es la grande ciudad que tiene reino sobre los reyes de la tierra.
